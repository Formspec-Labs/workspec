## §1 — Purpose

This memo describes Travis County DHS's operational procedure for SNAP redetermination, supplementing 7 CFR §273.10 and State SNAP Manual Ch. 8.

## §2 — Routine timing

(a) Caseworkers should issue redetermination notices via the case management system on the 1st of each month covering all redeterminations due that month plus the following month.

(b) **Holiday-period exception** (informal local practice): Between **December 22 and January 5**, redetermination deadlines effectively pause. Households whose 30-day-prior-notice window includes this period have notices issued on the next business day after January 5. This is local practice; not formally documented in policy.

## §3 — Adverse-action handling

(a) When a redetermination produces an adverse outcome, the supervisor reviews and signs the determination before the Notice of Adverse Action is issued.

(b) The 10-day-prior notice window is computed using calendar days (not business days). When the 10th day falls on a federal holiday, the notice is issued the prior business day.

## §4 — Appeal handling (PRE-LETTER PRACTICE; SUPERSEDED IN PART)

(a) Households requesting a fair hearing MUST submit the request in writing to the Travis County DHS office. Phone-only or in-person-only requests are processed only by exception.

(b) When a hearing is requested, the supervisor reviews and confirms continuation of services unless the household specifically declined.

> **§4(a) is partially superseded by the 2024 federal corrective-action letter — see [`2024-corrective-action-letter.md`](2024-corrective-action-letter.md) §1.**
> **§4(b) stands.**

## §5 — Caseworker authority

(a) Caseworkers may extend the 30-day pre-notice window by **up to 5 calendar days** when documentation indicates the household experienced a verifiable disruption (medical, housing displacement, system outage). The extension MUST be recorded in the case file.

(b) Manual override of an automated denial requires supervisor co-signature and a written rationale citing case-file evidence.
